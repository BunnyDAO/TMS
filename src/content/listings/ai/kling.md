## Getting Started

1. Visit [klingai.com](https://klingai.com) and create a free account to access the video generation platform.
2. Choose between text-to-video or image-to-video mode depending on your creative needs.
3. Write a detailed prompt describing your desired scene, including subject, action, environment, and camera movement.
4. Generate your video and download it in up to 1080p resolution for use in your projects.

## Key Features

- **Exceptional motion quality** produces some of the most realistic character movement and physical interactions in AI video.
- **Up to 2-minute videos** generates longer clips than most competitors, enabling more complex storytelling.
- **Image-to-video animation** brings still images to life with natural, physically plausible motion.
- **1080p output resolution** delivers high-definition video suitable for social media and professional content.
- **Free tier available** provides daily generation credits so users can try the platform without payment.
- **Lip sync and expression** generates realistic facial animations and speech-synchronized mouth movements.
