## Getting Started

1. Visit [midjourney.com](https://midjourney.com) and sign up for a subscription plan starting at $10/month.
2. Access Midjourney through the web app at [midjourney.com/imagine](https://midjourney.com/imagine) or via the Discord bot.
3. Type your image description (prompt) and Midjourney will generate four image variations within seconds.
4. Use upscale, variation, and remix tools to refine your favorite result to your exact specifications.

## Key Features

- **Industry-leading image quality** produces highly aesthetic, detailed images across artistic styles and photorealistic scenes.
- **Advanced prompt understanding** interprets complex descriptions including style references, lighting, mood, and composition.
- **Style and character references** let you maintain visual consistency by uploading reference images to guide generation.
- **Web-based editor** provides in-painting, out-painting, and region-specific regeneration for precise control.
- **Fast generation speeds** deliver four high-quality image options in under 30 seconds on standard plans.
- **Describe feature** analyzes uploaded images and generates text prompts to help you recreate similar styles.
