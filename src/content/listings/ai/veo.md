## Getting Started

1. Access Veo through Google's AI tools at [labs.google](https://labs.google) or via the Vertex AI platform.
2. Write a descriptive text prompt specifying the scene, camera angles, lighting, and mood you want.
3. Optionally upload a reference image to guide the visual style and content of the generated video.
4. Preview the generated clips and iterate on your prompt to refine the output to match your vision.

## Key Features

- **Cinematic video quality** produces detailed, high-resolution videos with professional-grade visual fidelity.
- **Advanced motion understanding** accurately simulates real-world physics, lighting dynamics, and natural movement.
- **Camera control directives** support specific cinematography instructions like drone shots, rack focus, and tracking.
- **Style transfer** can generate videos in specific artistic styles guided by reference images or text descriptions.
- **Google Cloud integration** available through Vertex AI for enterprise-scale video generation workflows.
- **SynthID watermarking** embeds invisible digital watermarks for responsible AI content identification.
