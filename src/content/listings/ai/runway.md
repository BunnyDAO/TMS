## Getting Started

1. Sign up at [runwayml.com](https://runwayml.com) and explore the free tier with limited generation credits.
2. Choose a generation mode: text-to-video, image-to-video, or video-to-video transformation.
3. Provide your input prompt or reference media and adjust generation settings like motion intensity and style.
4. Use Runway's built-in editor to trim, combine, and post-process generated clips into final productions.

## Key Features

- **Cinema-quality output** with Gen-4.5 producing some of the most visually consistent AI-generated video available.
- **Multiple generation modes** including text-to-video, image-to-video, video-to-video, and motion brush tools.
- **Motion Brush** lets you paint specific areas of an image to control where and how motion occurs.
- **Professional editing suite** includes green screen removal, inpainting, super slow-motion, and color grading tools.
- **Character consistency** maintains visual identity of subjects across multiple generated shots and scenes.
- **API access** enables developers to integrate Runway's generation capabilities into production workflows.
