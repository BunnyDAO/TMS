## Getting Started

1. Visit [suno.com](https://suno.com) and create a free account to receive daily generation credits.
2. Enter a text description of the song you want, including genre, mood, and topic, or write custom lyrics.
3. Suno will generate two full song variations complete with vocals, instrumentals, and structure.
4. Download your favorites, extend tracks, or use the custom mode to fine-tune lyrics, style, and structure.

## Key Features

- **Full song generation** creates complete tracks with vocals, harmonies, instrumentals, and professional mixing.
- **Any genre support** produces music across pop, rock, hip-hop, jazz, electronic, classical, and dozens of other styles.
- **Custom lyrics mode** lets you write your own lyrics and specify the exact musical style and arrangement.
- **Song extension** continues and extends generated tracks, allowing you to build longer compositions.
- **High audio quality** outputs radio-ready tracks at high bitrates suitable for sharing and publishing.
- **Cover and remix tools** transform existing songs into new styles while maintaining the core melody and lyrics.
