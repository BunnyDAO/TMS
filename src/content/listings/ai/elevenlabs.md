## Getting Started

1. Sign up at [elevenlabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io) and access the free tier with 10,000 characters per month.
2. Enter text in the Speech Synthesis tool and select from hundreds of pre-made voices or the default options.
3. Clone your own voice by uploading a short audio sample to create a custom voice profile.
4. Integrate via the API for production applications using the official Python, JavaScript, or REST APIs.

## Key Features

- **Ultra-realistic speech synthesis** produces natural-sounding voices virtually indistinguishable from human speech.
- **Instant voice cloning** creates a custom voice model from just a few minutes of audio sample.
- **29+ language support** generates natural speech across major world languages with native-sounding accents.
- **AI dubbing** automatically translates and dubs audio/video content while preserving the original speaker's voice.
- **Projects and long-form** handles audiobook-length content with consistent voice quality and natural pacing.
- **Developer API** provides low-latency streaming and batch generation for integrating voice into applications.
