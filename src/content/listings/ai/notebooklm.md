## Getting Started

1. Visit [notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com) and sign in with your Google account.
2. Create a new notebook and upload sources â€” PDFs, Google Docs, web links, YouTube videos, or text files.
3. Ask questions about your uploaded content and NotebookLM will answer with citations to specific sources.
4. Generate an Audio Overview to create a podcast-style discussion of your documents with AI hosts.

## Key Features

- **Audio Overview** generates engaging podcast-style conversations with two AI hosts discussing your uploaded content.
- **Source-grounded answers** responds only based on your uploaded documents, reducing hallucination and ensuring accuracy.
- **Multi-format sources** accepts PDFs, Google Docs, Slides, web URLs, YouTube videos, and plain text.
- **Inline citations** every answer includes clickable references to the specific source passages used.
- **Study guides and FAQs** automatically generates study materials, summaries, and frequently asked questions from your content.
- **Completely free** available at no cost with a Google account, including Audio Overview generation.
