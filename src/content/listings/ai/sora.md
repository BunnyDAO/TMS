## Getting Started

1. Access Sora through ChatGPT Plus or Pro at [chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com) or via [sora.com](https://sora.com).
2. Write a detailed text description of the video you want, including scene, camera movement, lighting, and style.
3. Choose your preferred resolution, aspect ratio, and duration settings before generating.
4. Use the storyboard feature to plan multi-shot sequences and maintain visual consistency across clips.

## Key Features

- **Photorealistic video generation** creates high-fidelity videos with natural motion, lighting, and physical interactions.
- **Up to 60-second clips** generates longer-form content compared to competitors, enabling more complex narratives.
- **Physics understanding** accurately simulates real-world physics including fluid dynamics, reflections, and object interactions.
- **Camera control** supports specific cinematographic directions like panning, tracking shots, and dolly zooms.
- **Storyboard mode** lets you plan and generate multi-scene videos with consistent characters and settings.
- **Image and video input** can extend, remix, or continue existing video clips and animate still images.
