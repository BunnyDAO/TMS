## Key Features

- **Cited answers** every response includes numbered source citations so you can verify and explore the underlying information.
- **Real-time web search** queries the internet live to provide up-to-date answers about current events and recent data.
- **Focus modes** let you target searches to Academic papers, YouTube videos, Reddit discussions, or specific domains.
- **Collections** organize your research into shareable folders for collaborative investigation of topics.
- **Follow-up questions** supports natural conversational research with context maintained across questions.
- **API access** enables developers to integrate Perplexity's search-augmented AI into their own applications.
