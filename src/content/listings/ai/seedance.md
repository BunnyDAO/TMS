## Getting Started

1. Visit [seedance.io](https://seedance.io) or use the Jianying/CapCut app.
2. Enter a text prompt describing your video.
3. Optionally upload a reference image or video for guided generation.
4. Generate up to 15-second clips with audio.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-Video** — Describe a scene and get a video
- **Image-to-Video** — Animate a still image with motion
- **Multimodal Input** — Combine text, image, audio, and video references
- **Audio-Video Joint Generation** — Synchronized audio and visuals
- **High Fidelity** — Industry-leading visual quality and coherence

## Ecosystem Connections

- Competes with **Sora**, **Runway**, **Kling**, and **Veo** in the AI video space
- Built by ByteDance's Seed team (same org behind TikTok's recommendation engine)
- Available through **CapCut** for global users
