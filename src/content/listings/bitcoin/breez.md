4. Use the built-in point-of-sale mode to accept Lightning payments at your business.

## Key Features

- **Breez SDK** an embeddable Lightning SDK that lets developers add Bitcoin payments to any application in hours.
- **Non-custodial wallet** users maintain full control of their funds with self-custodied Lightning channels.
- **Built-in point-of-sale** merchants can accept Lightning payments with a built-in POS interface and NFC support.
- **Podcast player** integrated podcast player with streaming sats (Value4Value) payments to creators.
- **Multi-platform SDK** available for React Native, Flutter, Swift, Kotlin, and Python for broad developer adoption.
- **LSP integration** connects to Breez's Lightning Service Provider for automatic channel management and inbound liquidity.
