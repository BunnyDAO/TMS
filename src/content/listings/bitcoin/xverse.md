## Getting Started

1. Install Xverse as a browser extension from [xverse.app](https://xverse.app) or download the mobile app for iOS/Android.
2. Create a new wallet and securely back up your 12-word recovery phrase in a safe offline location.
3. Fund your wallet with Bitcoin and explore the built-in gallery to view your Ordinals and BRC-20 tokens.
4. Connect to Bitcoin dApps and marketplaces by clicking the Xverse icon when prompted on supported sites.

## Key Features

- **Full Ordinals support** view, send, and receive Bitcoin Ordinals inscriptions with a built-in gallery and collection viewer.
- **BRC-20 and Runes** manage fungible tokens on Bitcoin including BRC-20 tokens and the newer Runes protocol.
- **Stacks integration** interact with Stacks smart contracts, STX tokens, and Bitcoin DeFi applications.
- **Lightning Network** send and receive instant, low-fee Bitcoin payments via integrated Lightning support.
- **Multi-platform** available as a browser extension (Chrome, Brave) and native mobile app (iOS, Android).
- **dApp browser** connect to the growing ecosystem of Bitcoin decentralized applications and marketplaces.
