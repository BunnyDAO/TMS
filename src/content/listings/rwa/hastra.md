## Getting Started

1. **Visit Hastra** — Go to hastra.io and explore the available private credit yield products offered on Solana.
2. **Connect a Wallet** — Connect a Solana-compatible wallet such as Phantom or Solflare to interact with the protocol.
3. **Review Yield Opportunities** — Browse available pools backed by institutional real-world lending facilitated through the Figure partnership.
4. **Deposit and Earn** — Provide liquidity to access sustainable yields sourced from off-chain private credit markets.

## Key Features

- **Real-World Asset Backing** — Yields are derived from institutional private credit loans, not purely speculative crypto activity.
- **Solana Infrastructure** — Built on Solana for fast, low-cost transactions compared to Ethereum-based RWA alternatives.
- **Figure Partnership** — Integrates with Figure Technologies, an established fintech lender, to source and manage real-world loan collateral.
- **Sustainable Yields** — Designed to offer more stable returns tied to traditional lending markets rather than token incentives.
- **DeFi Accessibility** — Brings institutional credit products on-chain, making them accessible to a broader DeFi audience.
- **Financial Innovation Focus** — Part of a broader mission by Hastra-Fi to develop novel use cases at the intersection of traditional finance and decentralized protocols.
