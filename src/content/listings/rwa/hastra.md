## Getting Started

1. **Visit the platform** — Go to hastra.io to explore available lending products and yield opportunities.
2. **Connect your wallet** — Use a Solana-compatible wallet such as Phantom or Solflare to access the protocol.
3. **Review available pools** — Browse institutional lending pools backed by audited real-world assets and assess yield options.
4. **Deposit and earn** — Allocate funds to a qualifying pool to begin earning yields sourced from real-world lending activity.

## Key Features

- **Solana-based infrastructure** — Built on Solana for fast, low-cost transactions suitable for institutional-scale finance.
- **Figure partnership** — Developed in collaboration with Figure, a fintech firm specializing in blockchain-based lending and financial services.
- **Real-world asset backing** — Yields are derived from audited, institutional-grade lending rather than purely crypto-native mechanisms.
- **Sustainable yield generation** — Focuses on stable, real-economy returns as an alternative to volatile DeFi incentive programs.
- **Institutional-grade assets** — Targets private credit markets traditionally accessible only to large institutional investors.
