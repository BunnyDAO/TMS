## Getting Started

1. Visit [securitize.io](https://securitize.io/blackrock) to learn about BUIDL eligibility and requirements.
2. Complete the Securitize investor onboarding process including KYC/AML verification and accreditation.
3. Fund your account and subscribe to the BUIDL fund to receive tokenized shares on Ethereum.
4. Monitor your position and accrued dividends through the Securitize platform dashboard.

## Key Features

- **BlackRock-managed** backed by the world's largest asset manager with $10T+ in assets under management.
- **U.S. Treasury backing** the fund invests in short-term U.S. Treasury bills and repurchase agreements.
- **Daily dividend accrual** token holders earn yield that accrues daily and is distributed as new tokens monthly.
- **On-chain settlement** tokenized shares settle on Ethereum, enabling 24/7 transfers and programmable finance.
- **Securitize platform** issued and managed through Securitize's SEC-registered transfer agent infrastructure.
- **Institutional scale** designed for qualified institutional investors with minimum investment thresholds.
