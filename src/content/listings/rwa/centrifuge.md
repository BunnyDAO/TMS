## Getting Started

1. Visit [app.centrifuge.io](https://app.centrifuge.io) to explore available investment pools and their underlying asset portfolios.
2. Complete the onboarding process for your chosen pool, including KYC verification and investor agreements.
3. Invest in a pool by depositing USDC or DAI to receive pool tokens representing your share of the credit portfolio.
4. Monitor pool performance, repayments, and yield through the Centrifuge app dashboard.

## Key Features

- **Real-world asset tokenization** converts invoices, mortgages, trade finance, and other credit assets into on-chain tokens.
- **Structured pools** pools are structured with senior (lower risk) and junior (higher risk) tranches for different risk profiles.
- **MakerDAO integration** Centrifuge pools serve as collateral vaults in MakerDAO, connecting RWA lending to DAI generation.
- **Transparent reporting** on-chain tracking of asset performance, repayments, and defaults for full investor visibility.
- **Multi-chain deployment** operates on Ethereum and its own Centrifuge Chain for specialized RWA functionality.
- **Institutional-grade compliance** KYC/AML verification, legal agreements, and regulatory frameworks for each pool.
