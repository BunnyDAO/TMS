1. Visit [app.wenrwa.com](https://app.wenrwa.com) and connect your Solana wallet (Phantom, Backpack, or Solflare).
2. Browse available RWA-linked tokens and trade with AI-powered strategies.

4. Use Solana's fast, low-cost transactions — no bridging to Ethereum required.
