## Getting Started

1. **Check eligibility** — USYC is a permissioned product available to qualified institutional investors; verify your jurisdiction and accreditation status before applying.
2. **Complete onboarding** — Work with Circle to complete KYC/AML and onboarding requirements necessary to access the permissioned smart contracts.
3. **Receive USYC tokens** — Once approved, receive USYC tokens representing your proportional claim on the underlying Treasury and Reverse Repo portfolio.
4. **Monitor on-chain** — Track holdings and yield accrual directly on-chain using the transparent smart contract infrastructure.

## Key Features

- **Short-Duration Treasuries** — Exposure to short-duration US Treasury bills and Reverse Repo agreements designed to minimize interest rate risk while generating yield.
- **Permissioned Smart Contracts** — Access is restricted to verified institutional participants through on-chain permissioning, maintaining regulatory compliance.
- **On-Chain Transparency** — Full portfolio transparency is available on-chain, allowing investors to verify holdings and operations without relying solely on off-chain reports.
- **Institutional-Grade** — Built for institutional use cases, offering the reliability and compliance standards expected by sophisticated investors.
- **Circle Backing** — Originally a Hashnote product, now operated under Circle, one of the leading regulated digital asset infrastructure companies.
- **DeFi Composability** — USYC tokens can potentially be integrated into broader DeFi protocols and workflows for institutional treasury management.
