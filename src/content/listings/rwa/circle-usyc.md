## Getting Started

1. **Check eligibility** — USYC is a permissioned product; confirm your institution meets Circle's KYC/AML and accreditation requirements.
2. **Apply for access** — Submit an institutional onboarding application through Circle's platform to gain whitelisted access to USYC.
3. **Connect your wallet** — Once approved, connect a whitelisted on-chain address to interact with the USYC token.
4. **Subscribe and manage** — Deposit eligible assets to receive USYC tokens representing your share of the underlying Treasury and Reverse Repo portfolio.

## Key Features

- **Tokenized US Treasuries** — Provides on-chain exposure to short-duration US Treasury bills, offering a low-risk yield-bearing instrument.
- **Reverse Repo exposure** — Includes Reverse Repo agreements alongside Treasuries for additional yield optimization within a conservative risk profile.
- **Permissioned access** — Restricted to verified institutional investors through Circle's onboarding process, ensuring regulatory compliance.
- **Circle infrastructure** — Built on Circle's established payments and stablecoin infrastructure, leveraging the same compliance and settlement rails.
- **Hashnote heritage** — Originally developed by Hashnote and acquired by Circle, bringing institutional-grade asset management expertise to the product.
- **On-chain transparency** — Token holdings and NAV are verifiable on-chain, enabling real-time auditability for institutional treasury management.
