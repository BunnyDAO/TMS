## Key Features

- **USDY yield-bearing stablecoin** a tokenized note backed by short-term U.S. Treasuries, accruing yield daily.
- **OUSG tokenized Treasuries** provides direct exposure to a portfolio of short-duration U.S. government bonds.
- **Multi-chain availability** USDY and OUSG are available on Ethereum, Solana, Arbitrum, Mantle, and other networks.
- **Institutional compliance** fully regulated with KYC/AML requirements and transparent reserve management.
- **DeFi composability** Ondo tokens are integrated into lending protocols, DEXs, and yield strategies across DeFi.
- **Regular attestations** third-party verification of underlying Treasury holdings ensures transparency and trust.
