## Getting Started

1. Visit [app.maple.finance](https://app.maple.finance) and explore available lending pools and their terms.
2. Complete the lender onboarding process including KYC/AML verification for eligible pools.
3. Deposit USDC into your chosen lending pool to start earning yield from institutional borrowers.
4. Monitor your lending position, interest accrual, and pool health through the Maple dashboard.

## Key Features

- **Institutional borrowers** pools lend to vetted institutional counterparties like trading firms and fintech companies.
- **Professional credit delegates** experienced underwriters manage pools and conduct due diligence on borrowers.
- **Higher yields** private credit pools typically offer higher returns than standard DeFi lending protocols.
- **Structured lending** pools feature defined terms, collateral requirements, and repayment schedules.
- **Multi-chain deployment** available on Ethereum and Solana for broad accessibility.
- **Transparent on-chain tracking** all loan terms, repayments, and defaults are tracked transparently on the blockchain.
