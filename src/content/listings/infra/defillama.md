## Getting Started

1. Visit [defillama.com](https://defillama.com) to explore TVL rankings, chain comparisons, and protocol data.
2. Use the chain comparison view to analyze TVL distribution and growth across different blockchains.
3. Explore the Yields page to find the best DeFi yield opportunities across all protocols and chains.
4. Access the free API at [defillama.com/docs/api](https://defillama.com/docs/api) to integrate DeFi data into your applications.

## Key Features

- **Comprehensive TVL tracking** monitors total value locked across 3000+ protocols on 200+ blockchains.
- **Yield aggregator** tracks and compares yield opportunities across all DeFi protocols for optimal farming strategies.
- **Stablecoin dashboard** monitors stablecoin supply, flows, and market share across all chains and issuers.
- **Protocol revenue and fees** tracks earnings data for DeFi protocols, providing fundamental analysis metrics.
- **Free, open-source API** provides programmatic access to all DeFi data with no API key required.
- **No ads or paywalls** completely free and open-source, funded by community contributions and maintained transparently.
