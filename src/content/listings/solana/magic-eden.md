## Getting Started

1. Visit [magiceden.io](https://magiceden.io) and connect your wallet for the chain you want to trade on (Phantom for Solana, MetaMask for Ethereum, etc.).
2. Browse trending collections, new launches, and top movers across supported chains.
3. Buy NFTs instantly at listed prices or make offers below the listing price.
4. List your own NFTs for sale by selecting them from your wallet and setting your desired price.

## Key Features

- **Multi-chain marketplace** trade NFTs on Solana, Ethereum, Bitcoin (Ordinals), Polygon, and Base from one platform.
- **Bitcoin Ordinals support** full marketplace for buying, selling, and inscribing Bitcoin Ordinals and BRC-20 tokens.
- **Launchpad** helps new NFT projects launch collections with minting pages and creator tools.
- **Collection offers** make offers on entire collections to quickly acquire floor-priced NFTs.
- **Rarity tools** built-in rarity rankings and trait filters to evaluate NFTs before purchasing.
- **Creator tools** provides royalty enforcement, collection analytics, and promotional features for NFT creators.
