## Getting Started

1. Visit [tensor.trade](https://tensor.trade) and connect your Solana wallet (Phantom or Backpack recommended).
2. Browse collections using real-time floor prices, volume charts, and holder analytics.
3. Place bids on individual NFTs or make collection-wide offers with customizable parameters.
4. Use the portfolio view to track your NFT holdings, profit/loss, and trading history.

## Key Features

- **Real-time market data** live floor prices, sales feeds, and volume charts updated in real-time for fast decision-making.
- **Collection-wide bids (cBids)** place offers across entire collections with trait filters and price curves.
- **AMM pools** automated market maker pools for NFT liquidity that enable instant buys and sells.
- **Advanced charting** TradingView-style charts for NFT collections with volume, floor price, and listing data.
- **Speed and performance** optimized for Solana's speed with instant transaction submission and confirmation.
- **Compressed NFT support** full trading support for Solana's compressed NFT standard for lower-cost digital collectibles.
