## Getting Started

1. Visit [app.aave.com](https://app.aave.com) and connect your wallet on your preferred network (Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, etc.).
2. Supply assets like ETH, USDC, or DAI to begin earning variable or stable interest rates.
3. Use your supplied assets as collateral to borrow other tokens at competitive interest rates.
4. Monitor your Health Factor to ensure your position remains safe from liquidation.

## Key Features

- **Supply and earn** deposit crypto assets to earn interest from borrowers, with rates adjusting based on utilization.
- **Overcollateralized borrowing** borrow assets against your supplied collateral with flexible repayment terms.
- **Flash loans** borrow any amount without collateral as long as it is repaid within the same transaction block.
- **Multi-chain deployment** available on Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, Base, Avalanche, and more.
- **GHO stablecoin** Aave's native decentralized stablecoin minted by borrowers using their Aave collateral.
- **Risk management** sophisticated liquidation mechanisms and risk parameters governed by the Aave DAO.
