## Getting Started

1. Visit [opensea.io](https://opensea.io) and connect your wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet).
2. Browse trending collections, explore categories, or search for specific NFTs by name or contract address.
3. Buy instantly with fixed-price listings, or place offers and bid in auctions.
4. Create and list your own NFTs directly on OpenSea without needing to deploy a smart contract.

## Key Features

- **Multi-chain support** trades NFTs across Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base, Avalanche, and more from a single interface.
- **Seaport protocol** open-source, decentralized marketplace protocol that powers all OpenSea trades with gas-optimized smart contracts.
- **Collection offers** place offers across entire collections or filter by specific traits to target exactly what you want.
- **Creator tools** mint and list NFTs with no upfront costs using lazy minting, set royalty percentages, and manage collections.
- **Pro trading features** real-time activity feeds, rarity rankings, floor price tracking, and advanced filtering for serious collectors.
- **OpenSea Studio** dashboard for creators to manage drops, allowlists, and on-chain royalty enforcement.
