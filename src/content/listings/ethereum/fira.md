## Getting Started

1. **Connect your wallet** — Visit fira.money and connect a compatible Ethereum wallet such as MetaMask or Rabby.
2. **Choose a market** — Browse available fixed-rate lending or borrowing markets and select the asset and term that fits your strategy.
3. **Lock your rate** — Supply assets to lend at a fixed rate or deposit collateral to borrow at a locked rate, securing predictable returns or costs.
4. **Manage your position** — Monitor your position onchain and exit or sell your yield at any time without waiting for term maturity.

## Key Features

- **Fixed-Rate Lending** — Supply assets and lock in a predetermined interest rate, eliminating exposure to variable rate fluctuations.
- **Fixed-Rate Borrowing** — Borrow against collateral at a fixed cost, giving borrowers predictable repayment obligations.
- **Sell Yield** — Monetize future yield upfront by selling it on the protocol before the lending term concludes.
- **Early Exit** — Close or transfer positions at any time onchain, providing liquidity and flexibility uncommon in fixed-rate protocols.
- **Permissionless Markets** — Anyone can participate without KYC or whitelisting, consistent with open DeFi principles.
- **Onchain Transparency** — All rate locks, positions, and exits are executed and verifiable directly on Ethereum.
