## Getting Started

1. Visit [app.uniswap.org](https://app.uniswap.org) and connect your wallet on Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Polygon, or other supported chains.
2. Swap tokens by selecting the input and output tokens, entering an amount, and confirming the transaction.
3. Provide liquidity to earn trading fees by depositing token pairs into Uniswap V3 concentrated liquidity pools.
4. Use the Uniswap mobile wallet app for a streamlined trading experience on your phone.

## Key Features

- **Permissionless trading** anyone can swap any ERC-20 token pair without registration, KYC, or centralized approval.
- **Concentrated liquidity (V3)** allows liquidity providers to allocate capital within specific price ranges for greater efficiency.
- **Multi-chain deployment** available on Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, Base, BNB Chain, and more.
- **Uniswap X** aggregates liquidity sources and uses Dutch auctions for optimal trade execution and MEV protection.
- **Liquidity provider rewards** earn trading fees proportional to your share of the pool from every swap.
- **Open-source protocol** with fully audited smart contracts and a governance system controlled by UNI token holders.
